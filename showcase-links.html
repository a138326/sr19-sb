Contact Staying Connected if you need help paying your bill

Financial challenges can arise from a sudden change in income, illness or other personal matters. Whatever your situation, get in touch. Our Staying Connected program could offer you some extra support. For more information, contact us on 1300 659 925.

Staying Connected is our hardship program for customer based in NSW, Qld, SA & WA. If you’re a Victorian customer, support is available as part of our Payment Support Victoria program.
